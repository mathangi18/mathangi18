### 🧠 Who Am I?

> *"I don't treat patients. I decode patterns in the signals they leave behind."*

Health Informatics researcher and data scientist, focused on **clinical deterioration prediction**, **synthetic patient modeling**, and the **ethics of AI in healthcare**. Currently building systems that could flag a sepsis event **hours before it happens**.
